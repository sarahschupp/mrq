Limited Lifetime Warranty

We stand behind every panel we manufacture. If it fails due to a defect in materials or workmanship, we make it right.

What's Covered

Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and installation conditions.

How Long

For the life of the original purchaser's ownership of the home. This warranty is non-transferable to subsequent owners.

Who It Covers

The original purchaser. Warranty is non-transferable to subsequent owners of the home.

This is a Limited Lifetime Warranty. It covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and proper installation.

Products Covered

The following Everlock Systems products are covered under this Lifetime Warranty:

  • Everlock Deluxe Vinyl Skirting
  • EverRock Vinyl Skirting
  • Thermal EverRock Insulated Skirting
  • Gorilla Insulated Skirting
  • Novik Faux Brick, Stone, and Stacked Stone Panels

Accessories (vents, access doors, rails, and hardware) are covered by a separate limited warranty. Contact us at 800-843-3336 for accessory warranty terms.

What This Warranty Covers

Everlock Systems warrants that the skirting products listed above are free from manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and installation conditions. If a covered product fails due to a manufacturing defect, Everlock Systems will, at its option, repair or replace the defective product.

What This Warranty Does Not Cover

This warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by improper installation (installation must follow Everlock's published instructions)
  • Damage caused by accident, misuse, neglect, vandalism, or acts of nature (including but not limited to hail, flooding, or wind events beyond the product's rated conditions)
  • Normal weathering, fading, or color change over time within industry-standard tolerances
  • Damage caused by contact with chemicals, solvents, or improper cleaning agents
  • Products that have been modified, altered, or repaired by parties other than Everlock Systems
  • Damage from lawn and landscape equipment (including string trimmers)
  • Cosmetic damage that does not affect the structural integrity or function of the product
  • Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To request warranty service, contact Everlock Systems directly:

Phone: 800-843-3336 (Monday-Friday, 8am-5pm Mountain Time)

Everlock Systems will evaluate the claim and respond within a reasonable time. If the claim is approved, we will ship replacement product at no charge. The customer is responsible for installation of replacement product.

Common Warranty Questions

Q Does the warranty transfer if I sell my home?
No. The warranty covers the original purchaser only. It is non-transferable.
Q Does the warranty cover color fading?
Normal weathering and gradual color change over time are not covered. If panels fade unevenly or prematurely due to a manufacturing defect in the UV coating, that may be covered. Contact us with photos and we'll evaluate.
Q I bought my skirting years ago and have a problem. Is it still covered?
Yes, as long as you are the original purchaser and the failure is due to a manufacturing defect. Call 800-843-3336 and we'll work through it with you.
