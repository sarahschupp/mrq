Common Questions

Trade Account FAQ

Is there a minimum order quantity for trade accounts?

No hard minimum. We work with contractors who do one or two jobs a year and park managers who order monthly. Call us and we'll talk through what makes sense for your volume.

Can you ship to multiple job sites on a single order?

Yes. We can split an order across multiple delivery addresses. Let us know when you're ordering and we'll coordinate the logistics with you.

Do you offer net terms for trade accounts?

We currently process orders by credit card. For established accounts with consistent volume, contact us to discuss payment arrangements that work for your business.

How long does shipping typically take?

Shipping times vary by product and delivery location. We'll give you a lead time estimate when you order. Most standard vinyl products ship within a few business days.

Can I get product samples before ordering for a project?

Yes. Order samples through our samples page or call us and we'll send you what you need to make the right call for the job.

Do your products meet FHA and VA skirting requirements?

Our skirting is designed to meet HUD Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards, which FHA and VA appraisers reference. Final inspection approval always rests with the lender and appraiser on that specific loan.
