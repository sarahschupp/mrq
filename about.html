Our Story

A Family Business Built on Quality and Trust Since 1981

We manufacture mobile home skirting the old-fashioned way, with pride, attention to detail, and a commitment to stand behind every product we make.

The Quatrochi family, Everlock Systems founders and current leadership

From a Handshake to a Multi-Generation Legacy

In 1981, Pete Quatrochi, a former U.S. Air Force Captain who had flown B-52s in Vietnam, and his partner Randy launched a skirting business on a handshake and a belief that manufactured home owners deserved better products at fairer prices. Pete had already proven himself in the RV awning industry. Skirting was next.

What started as a small Colorado operation quickly grew. By 1986, Everlock Systems, Inc. was formally established in Boulder, and the same year they introduced EverRock simulated stone skirting, a product that remains one of their bestsellers four decades later.

Pete's sons Phil and Nick Quatrochi joined the business over the years and now lead the company. Phil serves as Vice President; Nick is the current owner. The same family that started this on a handshake still answers the phone, still approves every product that ships, and still stands behind the warranty they have offered for over 45 years.

Our Promise

Two Plants. Zero Middlemen.

We manufacture at two U.S. plants, one in Birmingham, Alabama, and one in Booneville, Mississippi. When you order from SkirtingDirect.com, your panels come straight from those facilities to your door. No distributor. No retail markup. That is factory-direct pricing, and it has been our model since day one.

Sales and customer support are based in Boulder, Colorado. When you call 800-843-3336, you are talking to people who have spent years helping homeowners figure out exactly what they need.

45 Years of Product Innovation

  • 1981

    Everlock Systems founded in Boulder, Colorado by Pete Quatrochi. Everlock Deluxe Vinyl skirting introduced with the triple-lock panel system.

  • 1986

    Everlock Systems, Inc. formally incorporated. EverRock simulated stone vinyl launched.

  • 2001+

    Novik faux brick, stone, and stacked stone panels added to the product line. Thermal EverRock and Gorilla Insulated products introduced for cold-climate markets.

  • 2011

    SkirtingDirect.com launched as a direct-to-consumer channel, giving homeowners factory prices without the dealer markup.

  • Today

    Phil and Nick Quatrochi lead the company. The same core products, refined over decades, now ship from two U.S. plants to homeowners across the continental United States.
