# SkirtingDirect.com Photography Brief
*For agency, freelance photographer, or stock sourcing*

## Project Context

Everlock Systems is relaunching SkirtingDirect.com as a modern D2C e-commerce site. The visual style should be honest and grounded — real homes, real people, natural light. No overly styled shoots, no luxury aesthetics. The target customer owns a manufactured or mobile home and is practical, value-conscious, and proud of where they live.

## Visual Direction

Warm, natural lighting. Rural and suburban settings. Finished installs that look lived-in, not staged. Show the product doing its job — protecting the home, looking clean, blending into the landscape. Before/after pairs where possible. Kids and pets are a plus (safe, finished space under the home).

---

## Shot List by Section

### Hero (homepage)
3–5 options for A/B testing, landscape orientation, 1920px minimum width

- Finished single-wide with clean white or grey vinyl skirting, warm light, green grass
- Double-wide in a rural setting, stone or brick-look panels
- Family or homeowner standing near a freshly skirted home (candid, not posed)
- Close detail of Novik faux brick panel at the corner of a home
- Winter scene: insulated skirting on a home in snow

### Product Pages (one hero per product)

- **Everlock Deluxe Vinyl:** single-wide install, white or beige, clean suburban setting
- **EverRock Vinyl:** grey textured panels, side angle showing profile depth
- **Thermal EverRock Insulated:** cold-climate setting (frost, bare trees), emphasis on panel thickness
- **Gorilla Thermal Insulated:** under-home view showing installation depth, OR cold-weather exterior
- **Novik Stacked Stone:** upscale-looking home, stacked stone panels, landscaped yard
- **Novik Hand-Laid Brick:** brick-look panels, warm tones, close detail shot

### Color Swatches

Flat lay or wall-mounted, individual panels in each available color. Each panel photographed against a neutral background, consistent lighting. 1:1 ratio, min 800x800px. Current colors: White, Beige, Almond, Sandstone, Grey. Confirm full color lineup with Everlock before shoot.

### Installation / How It Works

- Someone measuring the perimeter of a home (regular clothes, not a uniform)
- Laying out panels before installation
- Snapping or sliding a panel into a track
- Finished install from ground level looking at the underside seal

### Real Homes (customer gallery)

These should come from actual customers where possible — use the $50 photo incentive program to build this library over time. For launch, use any available installer photos:

- Variety of home sizes and settings (South, Midwest, rural, suburb)
- Before/after pairs
- At least one double-wide
- At least one with Novik panels (premium tier differentiation)

### FHA/VA Section

Neutral, documentary-style image of a skirted home with clean curb appeal — nothing aspirational, just "this meets requirements."

## Technical Specs

|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| Hero images | 1920px | JPG | 16:9 or wider crop |
| Product heroes | 1200px | JPG | 3:2 or 4:3 |

| Installation steps | 900px | JPG | Square or 4:3 |

Deliver at 72 DPI web-optimized AND full-resolution originals. Name files descriptively: `product-everlock-deluxe-white-sideangle.jpg`, not `IMG_4821.jpg`.

## Stock Sourcing Notes (if a shoot isn't feasible at launch)

Acceptable sources: Getty, Shutterstock, Adobe Stock. Search terms that work: "mobile home skirting," "manufactured home exterior," "vinyl skirting install," "modular home landscaping." Avoid anything that looks like a luxury home, a construction site, or a staged interior. Homes in any stock photo should look like the homes your customers actually own.
