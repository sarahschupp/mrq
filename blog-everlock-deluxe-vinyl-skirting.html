What Everlock Deluxe Vinyl Looks Like

The Deluxe Vinyl features a clean, uniform horizontal panel profile that runs the entire perimeter of your home. This traditional skirting aesthetic works with any manufactured home style, from single-wide to double-wide, and blends seamlessly with standard home colors and trim.

The panels are made from UV-stabilized vinyl that maintains consistent color throughout the panel depth. Because the color is integral to the material, not a coating, it resists fading from Florida sun, mountain UV, or desert heat far better than surface-painted alternatives.

Available in multiple colors to match or complement your home's exterior. The simplicity of the horizontal profile makes the Deluxe Vinyl versatile enough to work with cottage-style homes, traditional single-wides, and even contemporary manufactured designs.

DIY Installation: The Most DIY-Friendly Product in the Lineup

Everlock Deluxe Vinyl is the simplest product we make to install. The horizontal panels slide directly into the top and bottom rails. No special tools required. No adhesives, no fasteners to worry about, no complex cuts or modifications. Most single-wide installations are completed in a single weekend by homeowners with basic handiness.

The installation process is straightforward: position your top rail (bolted to the home frame), slide panels in sequence from one end to the other, then secure the bottom rail. Access doors are sized to slide into the same rail system, so there are no complicated cutouts or special modifications needed for ventilation and water access.

For homeowners who prefer professional installation, many mobile home service contractors have experience with Everlock products and can complete installation within 1-2 days. Labor costs vary by region but typically range from $250 to $500 depending on perimeter and complexity.

Complete Kits vs Individual Panels

We offer both options. You can order individual panels and hardware a la carte, or you can order a complete kit sized to your home dimensions. The complete kit includes everything you need in one order: panels, top rail, bottom rail, access door, hardware, and ventilation components.

Complete kits are sized by home dimensions (length x width) and come with the exact panel count needed to wrap your home perimeter at your specified height above grade. This removes all guesswork about how many panels to order and eliminates the risk of under-ordering or over-ordering.

Both approaches use the same manufacturing, the same warranty, and the same quality. The kit is simply the convenience factor of having everything pre-counted and arriving in one shipment.

FHA and VA Loan Compliance

Everlock Deluxe Vinyl satisfies the skirting requirements outlined in HUD Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards. Full perimeter enclosure with an access door, durable material, and good condition all pass FHA and VA appraisals.

One key note: appraisers are checking for completeness, durability, and absence of visible defects. While Deluxe Vinyl meets all technical requirements, some appraisers view heavier products like Novik stone or brick with additional confidence due to their rigid construction and stone-like appearance. If you are in a high-stakes appraisal situation, confirm your lender's preferences in writing before ordering.

Important disclaimer: Final approval rests with your lender and appraiser, not with Everlock Systems. Always verify your lender's requirements in writing before ordering.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Everlock Deluxe Vinyl different from EverRock? +

Deluxe has a clean, uniform horizontal panel profile with a traditional skirting look. EverRock has a textured stone-surface finish that provides more visual interest and mimics the appearance of real stone or brick. EverRock is heavier and more durable, and costs more. Both carry the same Lifetime Warranty.

Is this a complete kit or individual panels? +

Both options are available. Complete kits include panels, rails, vents, access door, and hardware, sized to your home dimensions. Individual panels and hardware can be ordered a la carte. Complete kits remove guesswork and arrive in one shipment.

Can I install it myself? +

Yes. Everlock Deluxe Vinyl is the most DIY-friendly product in the lineup. Panels slide into top and bottom rails. Most homeowners complete a single-wide installation in one weekend. No special tools or expertise required. Full installation guide available at installation.html.

Does it come with a warranty? +

Yes. Every Everlock product, including Deluxe Vinyl, carries a true Lifetime Warranty on materials and workmanship. This is not a limited warranty with time restrictions or exclusions. It covers the original purchaser for the life of the product under normal use conditions.

Is there an insulated version? +

Not for Deluxe. The Deluxe Vinyl is non-insulated. If insulation matters for your climate or heating costs, see Thermal EverRock Insulated or Gorilla Insulated Skirting. Both options deliver R-value plus durability at higher price points.
