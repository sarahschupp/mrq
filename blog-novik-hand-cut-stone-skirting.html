Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone: The Middle of the Line, Top of the Look

Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone delivers the appearance of individual stones that have been cut and set by hand, with irregular edges and natural-looking variation across each panel. Unlike the simpler Stacked Stone look, Hand-Cut Stone features more detailed panel design with varying stone shapes and authentic texture. Unlike the rougher Field Stone, Hand-Cut maintains a refined, organized appearance that works with a wide range of home architectural styles.

The panels are molded directly from real stone surfaces, capturing the texture, color variation, and depth that make the product indistinguishable from actual hand-laid stone at any distance. Each panel is designed to interlock seamlessly with adjacent panels, eliminating visible seams while maintaining the natural stone aesthetic.

Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone is manufactured by Everlock Systems and sold factory-direct. Pricing starts at 3,710 dollars for a typical 14' x 66' home at 36 inches above grade (materials only, freight not included). This product is in the middle of the Novik line, positioned between the more affordable Stacked Stone and the more rustic Field Stone options.

What Sets Hand-Cut Stone Apart

The Novik line includes five distinct products, each with a different visual character. Premium Hand-Cut Stone occupies a sweet spot in the lineup. Here is what makes it unique.

Individual Stone Shapes with Irregular Edges

Each panel in Hand-Cut Stone features individual stone shapes cut with irregular edges that appear organic and hand-placed. The variation in stone size and positioning across the panel gives the skirting depth and visual interest that flat, uniform patterns cannot match.

Natural Color and Texture Variation

Novik panels are molded from real stone, which means the texture and color variation are authentic. You will see subtle differences in tone and finish across each panel and from panel to panel, just like real hand-laid stone. This eliminates the repetitive, artificial appearance of printed or painted finishes.

Refined Appearance That Works with Any Style

Unlike Field Stone, which has a more rustic, deliberately unfinished look, Hand-Cut Stone presents a curated, high-end aesthetic. It pairs well with traditional, contemporary, and transitional home styles. The refined stone appearance suggests craftsmanship and durability to appraisers and neighbors alike.

Moderate Installation Complexity

Hand-Cut Stone panels are heavier and more detailed than vinyl options, requiring professional installation in most cases. However, they are not as complex as some custom stone applications. Proper installation typically takes one to two days for a standard manufactured home.

Installation Overview

Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone installation is more involved than vinyl skirting due to the weight and complexity of the panels. Professional installation is recommended.

Typical Installation Timeline

A standard 14' x 66' home typically requires one to two days of professional installation, depending on site conditions, anchoring requirements, and the presence of obstacles.

What Installation Includes

  • Measurement and panel layout to minimize seams and ensure proper alignment
  • Securing the top rail to the home frame with bolts or lag screws
  • Panel placement and interlocking according to manufacturer specifications
  • Installation of trim pieces, corners, and the access door
  • Securing bottom rail and ensuring proper water drainage clearance

DIY vs. Professional

While some Everlock vinyl products can be installed by experienced homeowners, Novik Hand-Cut Stone installation should be handled by professionals. The weight of the panels, the precision required for proper alignment, and the need for secure anchoring justify professional labor. Proper installation ensures the product's durability and warranty coverage.

Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone: Common Questions

Is the stone texture realistic? +

Yes. Novik panels are molded directly from real stone surfaces, capturing authentic texture, color variation, and natural imperfections. At any distance, the panels are visually indistinguishable from hand-laid natural stone. Viewers would have to touch the panels to realize they are manufactured composite rather than actual stone.

Does it need sealing or regular maintenance? +

No. The color and texture are an integral part of the molded composite panel. They do not require sealing, staining, or refinishing. To clean the panels, simply rinse with water and a soft brush. The finish holds up to UV exposure, humidity, and weather without degradation or color loss.

How long will it last? +

Novik Premium Hand-Cut Stone is backed by a Lifetime Warranty on materials and workmanship under normal use conditions. The rigid composite construction resists cracking, warping, fading, and deterioration from humidity, salt air, UV exposure, and freeze-thaw cycles. Many installations have performed flawlessly for over 20 years.

Can I see a sample before ordering? +

Yes. We offer samples of all five Novik products so you can compare colors, textures, and finishes side by side. Seeing samples in person and in natural light is the best way to choose the product and color that matches your home's style. Order samples at skirtingdirect.com.

How does it differ from Dry Stacked Stone? +

Premium Hand-Cut Stone features individually cut irregular stone shapes that appear organic and hand-placed, with natural color and texture variation. Dry Stacked Stone has a more contemporary aesthetic with tightly fitted horizontal stone patterns and a more uniform appearance. Dry Stacked is the most premium product in the Novik line and commands a higher price. Hand-Cut Stone offers a beautiful estate-quality look at a more moderate price point.
