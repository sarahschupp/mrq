Common Questions

Things We Hear Before People Order

I found cheaper skirting on Amazon. Why should I pay more here?
Look at what you are actually buying. Most skirting sold through resellers is imported with no real warranty and no US support if something goes wrong. Our pricing is factory-direct, which is often lower than what you find on Amazon once you account for the full kit. Call us and we will give you a real comparison.
My local park dealer offered to install skirting for me. Should I just go with them?
A local installer can still use Everlock materials. You can order factory-direct from us and hand the product to whoever is doing the install. The installer only charges you for labor, not a materials markup on top of their markup. Ask your installer how much they are charging for materials, then compare to our prices.
Home Depot carries skirting. What is the difference?
Big-box stores typically carry one or two standard vinyl skirting options in white. They do not carry insulated products, stone panel systems, or complete kits. Their staff cannot advise you on FHA/VA requirements or help you figure out what your specific home needs. And the pricing includes a retail markup.
I am worried about ordering something I have never seen in person.
Samples are available for purchase. Call 800-843-3336 or fill out the samples request form and we will send you physical color samples before you commit to an order. You can see the material, feel the thickness, and confirm the color matches your home before anything ships.
What if I order the wrong amount?
Call us before you order. We help customers figure out quantities every day. Give us your home length, width, and height above grade and we will tell you exactly what you need, including how many vents, corners, and access doors to include. Getting the right amount the first time is part of what we do.
Is the lifetime warranty really unlimited?
It is a true lifetime limited warranty on materials and workmanship under normal use and conditions. It is limited to the original purchaser. It covers defects in how the product was made, not damage from improper installation or unusual conditions. Full terms are on our warranty page.
