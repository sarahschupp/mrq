Skirting for FHA or VA Loan Approval?

Most FHA/VA lenders require skirting that encloses the full perimeter with proper ventilation and an access door. Our complete kits are built to meet these general requirements. Add an access door to your kit and you are in good shape. Always confirm specific requirements with your lender.
