Frequently Asked Questions

45 years of helping homeowners. Here are the answers we give most.

Shipping & Delivery

How are your products shipped?
+
Most skirting panels are too long for UPS or FedEx, so they ship via LTL (less-than-truckload) freight on a semi-truck. Vents and doors ship separately via UPS. Shipping costs depend on your location and what you order. You can estimate your cost before checkout using the calculator on our website.
Do you ship to my address?
+
We ship to residential addresses in the continental United States. The freight carrier needs to be able to access your location with a semi-truck (18-wheeler). We do not currently ship to Alaska, Hawaii, Canada, Mexico, or internationally.
Will I need to unload the shipment myself?
+
Yes. To keep your shipping cost as low as possible, you are responsible for unloading. The panels can be unpackaged and carried in a few pieces at a time. If you need special delivery services (liftgate, driver unloading), you will pay those fees directly to the carrier at delivery.
What do I do if my order arrives damaged?
+
Inspect everything before signing the delivery receipt. If anything is damaged or missing, note it on the receipt, get an exception number from the driver, and call us at 800-843-3336. We'll handle the freight claim and get you replacement product. Do not sign and accept without noting damage, because once you do, we cannot file a claim on your behalf.
Can I cancel my order?
+
Yes, if we receive your cancellation request before the product has shipped. Once it's on the truck, we cannot cancel the order.
How long does delivery take?
+
Everlock Deluxe, EverRock, Gorilla, and Thermal EverRock generally ship within 3 business days and arrive within 7-10 business days after that. Novik brick and stone products ship approximately 5 business days after your order, with an additional 5-8 days in transit. Certain Novik colors may take up to 15 business days to ship. We cannot guarantee specific arrival dates due to carrier variables.
Can I pick up my order at the factory?
+
Our factories do not allow customer pickups due to daily shipping volumes. You may be able to arrange a pickup at the freight carrier's local terminal, but policies vary by carrier.

Products & Ordering

What is your most economical skirting option?
+
Everlock Deluxe Vinyl is our most affordable product, and still a premium skirting with .040" thickness. It's our most popular option for good reason.
Do you sell complete skirting kits?
+
Yes. We sell complete kits with panels, rails, and hardware, sized for standard manufactured home dimensions. Visit our Complete Kits page to find the right kit for your home size and get instant pricing.
How do I know how much skirting to order?
+
Use our online calculator. You'll need your home's length, width, and the average height off the ground. If you have an unusual setup, such as large height variations, decks, or additions, call us at 800-843-3336 and we'll calculate it for you.
How do I match the skirting color to my home?
+
Order a sample. Colors look different on screens, and the only reliable way to check is to hold the actual panel against your home. Samples of all products are available on our website.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
+
Thermal EverRock, Gorilla, and EverRock orders must be at least 10 panels. Everlock Deluxe minimum is 14 panels (preferably a full rail kit). We recommend ordering a few extra panels, because the cost of a second small shipment exceeds the cost of a couple extra panels up front.
Can I reuse skirting if I move my home?
+
In some cases, portions may be reusable, but generally no. Every home setup is unique in terms of exact dimensions and ground clearance. Skirting can also be damaged when a home is moved.
Can your products be used on conventional homes?
+
Our products are designed for manufactured and mobile homes, but many customers have used them creatively on conventional home projects, particularly the Novik faux stone and brick panels.
Do you offer discount coupons or financing?
+
We do not currently offer discount coupons. Promotions are sent to newsletter subscribers. For financing, we accept PayPal, which offers its own financing options. You can also split payment between two credit cards by calling us at 800-843-3336.

Installation

Can I install skirting myself?
+
Yes, if you're comfortable with basic home improvement projects and the safe operation of a skill saw. Our complete installation instructions are at skirtingdirect.com/installation. If you're not confident, we recommend finding a local installer. Search "mobile home installer" or "mobile home services" in your area.
Do you install skirting?
+
We manufacture and sell direct, so we don't install. To find an installer near you, search for mobile home services in your area.
Do you sell framing materials?
+
No. Framing materials are widely available at your local home improvement store.
How do I access underneath my home after skirting is installed?
+
We sell access doors and access door conversion kits specifically for this purpose.
How much ventilation do I need?
+
A common rule of thumb is one square foot of ventilation per 150 square feet of crawl space. But local codes vary, so check your local requirements before installing. Compliance with local codes is the homeowner's responsibility.

FHA/VA & Compliance

Does my skirting need to meet FHA or VA requirements?
+
If you're buying or refinancing a manufactured home with an FHA or VA loan, your lender will likely require skirting that meets HUD standards. Our products are manufactured to meet general HUD guidelines for skirting. We strongly recommend confirming specific requirements with your lender or appraiser before ordering, because final approval rests with them, not us.
What does FHA/VA-compliant skirting look like?
+
Generally, compliant skirting must enclose the entire perimeter of the home, be durable and weather-resistant, include proper ventilation, and include at least one access door. Our Everlock Deluxe Vinyl, EverRock, and insulated products all meet these general requirements. Our full FHA/VA resource page covers the specific checklist.
Which Everlock product is recommended for FHA/VA compliance?
+
Everlock Deluxe Vinyl is the most commonly specified product for FHA/VA compliance. It meets HUD material and durability standards, is widely accepted by lenders, and is the most cost-effective option. Call us at 800-843-3336 if your lender has specific questions.
