Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take? +
It depends on the product. Everlock Deluxe, EverRock, Gorilla Insulated Skirting, and Thermal EverRock typically ship within 3 business days and arrive within 7 to 10 business days from your order date. Most Novik brick and stone products ship within approximately 5 business days. Certain Novik colors ship in approximately 15 business days. Transit time after shipment is typically 5 to 8 additional business days. We will send you a tracking number as soon as your order ships.
How are products shipped? +
Most skirting products are too large for UPS or FedEx and ship via LTL (less than truckload) freight on a semi-truck. Vents and doors ship separately via UPS, except when ordered with EverRock (those ship together). Once your order ships, we will email you the carrier name and tracking number so you can schedule your delivery directly with the freight company.
Do I have to be there when my order arrives? +
Yes. You must be present to accept, inspect, and sign for your delivery. The freight carrier will contact you in advance to schedule a delivery window. Do not sign the delivery receipt until you have inspected the shipment for damage or missing items.
Do I have to unload the truck myself? +
Yes. To keep your shipping cost as low as possible, unloading is your responsibility. Skirting panels can be unpackaged at the truck and carried a few at a time. If you need liftgate service or other special delivery arrangements, those are available directly from the freight carrier for an additional fee paid at delivery.
What if my order arrives damaged or has missing items? +
Inspect everything before signing the delivery receipt. If anything is damaged or missing, note it on the receipt and get an exception number from the driver before they leave. Then call us at 800-843-3336 right away. We will file the freight claim and arrange replacement product or credit. If you sign the receipt without noting damage, it becomes very difficult to recover losses after the driver leaves.
Can I pick up my order from your factory? +
No. Due to daily shipping volume, our plants do not allow customer pickup. You may be able to arrange pickup at the freight carrier's local terminal, but terminal pickup policies vary by carrier. Call us and we can advise you once your order is placed.
Can I cancel my order? +
Yes, if you contact us before your order has shipped. Once it has shipped, we cannot cancel or reverse the order. Call 800-843-3336 as soon as possible if you need to cancel.
Where do you ship? +
We ship throughout the contiguous United States (the lower 48 states). We do not ship to Alaska, Hawaii, Canada, Mexico, or any international addresses. Call us at 800-843-3336 if you have questions about delivery to a specific location.

Products & Ordering

What is your most popular product? +
Everlock Deluxe Vinyl is our bestseller. It offers a strong balance of price, durability, and ease of installation for most homeowners.
How do I choose the right color? +
We strongly recommend ordering samples before you buy. Colors look different on a screen than they do in person. Hold the sample against your home siding in natural light for the most accurate match.
Do you offer complete kits? +
Yes. Our Complete Kits include panels, top and bottom rails, corner trim, and hardware, all sized for your home. It is the simplest and most popular way to order so nothing is missing when you are ready to install.
Can I order just the panels, without a full kit? +
Yes. You can order panels, rails, hardware, and accessories individually if you need replacements or want to customize your order. Call us and we will help you figure out exactly what you need.
Are there minimum order quantities? +
Yes. For EverRock, Gorilla Insulated Skirting, and Thermal EverRock, the minimum order is 10 panels. For Everlock Deluxe Vinyl, we prefer you order a full rail kit (the minimum is 14 panels). If you are unsure how many panels you need, call us at 800-843-3336 and we will help you calculate the right quantity. Ordering short means paying freight twice, so it is worth getting it right the first time.
What accessories do you sell? +
We carry Foundation Vents, EZ Access Doors, Everlock EZ Storage, and Squeek No More floor squeak eliminator, plus all the hardware you need for a complete installation.
What is the difference between vinyl and insulated skirting? +
Standard vinyl skirting provides a clean finished look and weather protection. Insulated options like Thermal EverRock and Gorilla Insulated Skirting add a layer of rigid foam inside the panel, which helps protect pipes in freezing temperatures and can reduce energy costs. If you live in a colder climate or have had frozen pipe problems, insulated skirting is worth the upgrade.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept major credit cards. PayPal financing is also available at checkout. If you need to split your payment between two credit cards, that must be done by phone. Call us at 800-843-3336, Monday through Friday, 9am to 4pm Mountain Time, and we will process a split payment for you. We do not offer discount coupons.
Can I reuse my skirting if I move my home? +
Generally no. Skirting panels are cut to fit your home's specific dimensions and installation height. Reusing them on a different home or lot is usually not practical. If you move your home, plan to order new skirting sized for the new site.

Installation

Can I install this myself? +
Yes. Many of our customers install their own skirting over a weekend. We provide clear written instructions. If you prefer to hire someone, search for a mobile home installer in your area.
What tools do I need? +
Basic tools: circular saw, drill, level, and a ladder. Complete kits include all the screws and hardware you need.
How do I measure my home for skirting? +
You need the total linear footage around your home's perimeter and the height from the ground to the bottom of your home (measured at its highest point). Our team can walk you through it over the phone. Call 800-843-3336, Monday through Friday, 9am to 4pm Mountain Time.

FHA/VA & Lender Requirements

Does your skirting meet FHA and VA requirements? +
Our skirting is manufactured to HUD Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards and is accepted by many FHA and VA lenders. That said, final approval rests with your lender and appraiser, not with Everlock Systems. We recommend confirming requirements with your lender before ordering.
Do I need an access door for FHA or VA approval? +
Most FHA and VA loans require at least one access door in the skirting for utility and inspection access. We sell EZ Access Doors designed to work with our skirting systems. Check with your lender to confirm what is required for your specific loan.
